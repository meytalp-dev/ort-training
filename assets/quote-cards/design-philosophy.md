# Luminous Testimony

## A Visual Philosophy for Social Proof in Education

### Movement Manifesto

Luminous Testimony is the art of making human voices glow. Not through decoration, but through *reverent emptiness* — vast fields of soft, breathable color that let a single sentence land with the weight of truth. Every quote card is a shrine to one person's experience, treated with the care of a museum placard beside a masterwork.

### Form & Space

The canvas is predominantly air. Generous margins — 15% on each side — create a sanctuary of calm. The quote text occupies the center-right of the composition (honoring Hebrew RTL flow), floating in a sea of muted gradient. A single geometric accent — a translucent circle, a whisper of a line — anchors the composition without competing. The Learni logo sits small and precise in its corner, a quiet signature of provenance.

### Color & Material

The palette draws from the Learni identity: soft teal (#89CFF0), sage green (#9DB5A0), warm sand (#F0E6D3), and a deep anchor (#3A3A50). Each card uses one dominant hue as a gradient wash — never flat, always subtly alive with a 5-10% luminosity shift. The text lives in near-black (#2A2A40) against this glow, with the author's name in a secondary tone. This is work that looks as though a master colorist spent hours calibrating each value.

### Typography & Hierarchy

Two typefaces only: Heebo Bold for the quote body (large, commanding, 1.4 line-height for breath between lines), and Heebo Regular for the attribution (smaller, lighter, understated). Quotation marks are rendered as oversized decorative glyphs — 120pt, in a 10% opacity version of the primary color — positioned as architectural elements rather than punctuation. Every kerning decision, every weight choice, reflects the obsessive attention of someone at the pinnacle of typographic craft.

### Rhythm & System

The five cards form a cohesive series — each with the same structural DNA but a different color soul. Card 1 (featured): teal gradient. Card 2: sage green. Card 3: warm gold. Card 4: soft lavender. The rating badge ("9.0/10") appears as a minimal pill shape, positioned consistently across the series. This systematic variation creates visual interest when displayed as a carousel while maintaining unmistakable brand unity.

### Craftsmanship Standard

Every element must appear labored over. No default shadows. No generic gradients. The subtle noise texture overlay (2% opacity) adds the feel of printed paper. The rounded rectangle frames (border-radius: 24px) echo the Learni design system. This is design that whispers expertise — the kind that makes viewers pause their scroll because something feels *different*, *considered*, *real*.
